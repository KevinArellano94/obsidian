### Our approach

Security-first means completely eliminating passwords and other phishable factors, and validating both users and their devices as separate but equally-important criteria necessary for authentication

### Our scope

Security-first means deeper approach to access security than traditional tools, including always-on posture monitoring (even for unmanaged devices), advanced cryptographic authentication and continuous re-certification for both users and devices.

### Our team

Security-first means access to leaders in cryptography, endpoint security, and third-party risk management, that boast a deep repository of patents and real-world experience. From answers to tough questions, to practical advice, the best in the biz is here to help, 24/7.
